About Me

I am a first-year PhD candidate under the supervision of Louise Budzynski and Laurent Massoulié, working in the ARGO research team. My work focuses on studying Bayesian Inference problems with structured signal by using statistical physics methods.

Before that, I graduated from the international track of the MSc in Physics of Complex Systems offered jointly by Politecnico di Torino, SISSA, ICTP, Sorbonne Université, Université Paris Cité and Université Paris-Saclay. My master thesis "Statistical inference with correlated disorder: the planted spin glass" was supervised by Louise Budzynski and Alfredo Braunstein.
